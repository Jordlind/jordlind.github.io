A robust porter with notes of coffee, dark chocolate and a hint of liquorice.
Brewed when the leaves fall and the evenings grow dark.

## Recipe (20 litres)

### Malt

| Malt | Amount | Share |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Pale Ale malt | 4.2 kg | 78% |
| Chocolate malt | 0.5 kg | 9% |
| Roasted barley | 0.3 kg | 6% |
| Crystal 60 | 0.4 kg | 7% |

### Hops

- **East Kent Goldings** – 30 g at 60 min
- **Fuggles** – 15 g at 15 min

### Yeast

- SafAle S-04 English Ale

### Mash

Mash in at **67 °C** for 60 minutes for a slightly fuller body.

### Fermentation

Ferment at 18–20 °C for about two weeks. Let the porter condition in the bottle
for at least three weeks — the flavours round out and mellow with time.

> Cheers to the dark evenings. 🍺
