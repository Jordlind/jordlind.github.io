With an irresistibly smooth texture and full roasted chocolate flavours, the Oat Stout is an exquisite, comforting beer to enjoy through the dark winter months.

The rolled oats give the beer its characteristic silky body. A generous addition of chocolate malt and roasted barley builds the dark colour and bitter-chocolate finish.

> Tip: The oats make the beer prone to oxidation — avoid splashing when bottling. 🍺
