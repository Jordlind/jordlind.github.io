name: Belgian Tripel Original
style: Belgian Tripel
tagline: Deceptively smooth and golden — strong, dry and dangerously drinkable.
---

Thanks to a less complex malt character than its cousin the Dubbel, the Belgian Tripel finishes fresh and crisp. Despite the high alcohol it never tastes heavy — golden, sparkling and dangerously easy to drink.

This is the original Jordlind recipe — the one that started it all. A simple grain bill of Belgian pilsner malt and a touch of Caramunich I, fermented long and cold for a clean, golden result.

> Strong, golden and deceptively gentle. Skål! 🍺
