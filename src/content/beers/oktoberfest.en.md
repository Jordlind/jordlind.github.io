This German-style amber lager follows tradition: brewed in the cool of spring, lagered in a cold cellar through the summer months, and enjoyed come autumn at Oktoberfest.

Rich malt character from Vienna and Munich malts gives it a warm amber colour and a smooth, bready finish. Noble hops keep the bitterness clean and restrained.

> Best enjoyed after a proper cold lagering period. Patience is the ingredient that makes it great. 🍺
